Make your way to UTSA.

If you’re a Lone Star College STEM student planning to transfer to UTSA, we want to support your next steps. Use the interest form so we can understand how many students are coming in and share scholarship updates, events, and reminders.

I’m a Lone Star STEM student → I’m interested View Official Scholarship Details (UTSA)
Submitting our interest form is not the official scholarship application. It helps our partnership team track interest and share resources early.

What this site is for

  • Track how many Lone Star STEM students plan to transfer to UTSA
  • Share scholarship reminders and next steps
  • Answer questions and route students to the right UTSA resources

For full eligibility rules and official instructions, use the UTSA page linked above.

Quick next steps

  1. Submit the Interest Form on this site.
  2. Apply to UTSA as a transfer student (official UTSA process).
  3. Once admitted, apply through the UTSA Scholarship Hub (per UTSA instructions).

Exact steps can change by term—always follow the official UTSA page for final instructions.

Who should submit the interest form?

Any Lone Star College student pursuing a STEM pathway who is considering UTSA—whether you’ve applied yet or not.

  • Not applied yet? Still submit.
  • Applied and waiting? Submit.
  • Admitted and planning to enroll? Definitely submit.

What happens after you submit?

Our team may email you with partnership updates, deadlines, info sessions, and reminders related to transfer steps and scholarship processes.

Please do not submit sensitive information (SSN, banking info, full address, etc.).

Need help right now?

If you have a question that isn’t answered on the UTSA scholarship page, use our contact page and we’ll point you to the right office or resource.
